About me:

Hi, I'm Anabel, and I just love baking cakes. Since I was a kid, I've been whipping up sweet treats for my family and friends. In 2020, I brought my baking passion to Stockholm, helping families get the cakes they've always dreamed of. From simple designs to mind-blowing creations that make you question, "Is this really a cake?"—I'm all about adding a bit of sweetness to your special moments.
